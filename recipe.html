My Favorite Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Add the eggs and milk, and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
